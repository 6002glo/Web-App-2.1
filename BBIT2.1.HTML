Welcome to Anime World — your guide to the greatest animated stories ever told.

What is Anime?

Anime is a style of animation from Japan. It covers every genre — action, romance, fantasy, horror, and more.

Popular Anime Series

Some of the most loved series include Naruto, Attack on Titan, One Piece, and Demon Slayer.

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a boy who becomes a demon slayer to save his sister Nezuko.

Fun Fact

Demon Slayer's movie, Mugen Train, became the highest-grossing anime film of all time in 2021.
